ANOVA crash course

sst - sums of square total
sst = n x variance

anova - analysis of variance

ssm - model sums of squares (how much the model explains)
sse - error sums of squares (how much the model doesn't explain)

an f test is a type of "omnibus test", a test that contains many items or groups
f tests can show that there is significance somewhere, but it can't tell you where
so t tests pair off two conditions at a time
